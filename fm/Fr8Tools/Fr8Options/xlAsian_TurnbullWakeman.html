xlAsian_TurnbullWakeman

This function calculates the fair value or risk statistics (delta, gamma, vega...) for Asian style options on freight using the Turnbull & Wakeman approximation model. This model is ultimately based on the generalised Black-Scholes model and is widely used as an analytic approximation for pricing geometric average Asian options. (For further evaluation of the various option pricing models offered by Fr8Tools, please refer to the document “Option Pricing Model Comparison”)
Argument Description Sample Data Switch
optType Type of option (call/put) 1 1=Call, 2=Put
FFAprice Price of the underlying FFA contract 20000
realAvg Realised average price since the beginning of the average period 0.00
strike Strike price 22000
timeMat1 Original time to maturity 0.50
timeMat2 Remaining time to maturity 0.25
timeBegAvg Time to the beginning of the average period 0.00
intRate Risk-free interest rate 4% or 0.04
costCarry Cost of carry 2% or 0.02
vol Volatility (annualised) 40% or 0.40
reqResult Required result 1 1=Price, 2=Delta, 3=Gamma, 4=Vega, 5=Theta, 6=Rho

References
  • Turnbull, S.M., and L.M. Wakeman (1991), “A Quick Algorithm for Pricing European Average Options”, Journal of Financial and Quantitative Analysis, 26, 377-389.
