Functions

Fr8Tools provides a suit of Excel functions, which can be invoked using the standard Excel function wizard.
The Fr8Tools modules are specifically designed to offer a wide range of calculations relating to their area of specialisation. For Example, the Fr8Options module provides formulas for the calculation of freight options, such as fair value, implied volatility, historical volatility,Greeks, etc.
