xlAsian_TurnbullWakeman_IV

This function calculates the implied volatility for Asian style options on freight under the Turnbull & Wakeman approximation model. The function takes the price of the option as given (argument: Price) and recovers the volatility parameter which generates this price under the Turnbull & Wakeman model.
Argument Description Sample Data Switch
optType Type of option (call/put) 1 1=Call, 2=Put
FFAprice Price of the underlying FFA contract 20000
realAvg Realised average price since the beginning of the average period 0.00
strike Strike price 22000
timeMat1 Original time to maturity 0.50
timeMat2 Remaining time to maturity 0.25
timeBegAvg Time to the beginning of the average period 0.00
intRate Risk-free interest rate 4% or 0.04
costCarry Cost of carry 2% or 0.02
price Price of the option 2500

References
  • Turnbull, S.M., and L.M. Wakeman (1991), “A Quick Algorithm for Pricing European Average Options”, Journal of Financial and Quantitative Analysis, 26, 377-389.
  • Press, W., S. Teukolsky, W. Vetterling, and B. Flannery (1992), Numerical Recipes in C, Cambridge University Press.
