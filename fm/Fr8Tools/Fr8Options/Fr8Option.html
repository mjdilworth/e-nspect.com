Fr8Option

The first element in the Fr8Tools product suite is the Fr8Option module.
This is specifically designed to offer a wide range of calculations relating to freight options, such as fair value, implied volatility, historical volatility, Greeks, etc. Furthermore, Fr8Options includes a complete set of Excel functions and workbook solutions for forward curve building. A unique feature of Fr8Option is the pricing model which has been developed by FreightMetrics for pricing freight options more accurately by simulating off the full forward curve of freight rates.
