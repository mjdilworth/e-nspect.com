Fr8Tools Environment

Fr8Tools has been specially designed to operate within the familiar Microsoft Excel environment. By taking this approach FreightMetrics has reduced the earning curve. There are no proprietary interfaces to learn. Everything is where you would expect to find it within the standard Excel environment.

Fr8Tools can be broken down into four main areas, Excel Toolbar, Excel Functions, Excel Spreadsheets and the Data Manager
