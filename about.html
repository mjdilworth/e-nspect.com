About e-nspect

e-nspect is an IT consultancy focusing on providing full product life cycle services within the three core fields of Network, Systems and Software Engineering. e-nspect is a visionary organisation delivering best of breed solutions to business problems using the most appropriate technologies available. Whilst e-nspect is a champion of the open source movement, actively pushing the adoption of projects such as Kannel, Tomcat, MySQL and OpenLDAP into rigid telco environments, it is just as much at home implementing and supporting propriety solutions from vendors such as Microsoft, Sun, BEA and Cisco.

Over the years other like minded people have joined e-nspect and together we deliver the best at reasonable prices.
